## Notes

This section records why I made these things.

- Beginning  
  I joked with a friend that I was going to make tutorials, and they were very supportive.  
  To be honest, I knew very little back then, so I thought I would just write casually. Nobody would read it anyway.

- Articles  
  Then I made the blog site. It was originally called `VRChat Tutorials`. While checking references during writing, I realized how limited my knowledge actually was, so I ~~changed the site name overnight~~.

- Videos  
  At first I only wanted to write articles, but some things are hard to show with text alone, so I tried making videos.  
  I did not expect anyone to watch them, and after that the articles became a little less casual.  
  Reading comments and seeing responses from others made me feel happy ~~and also made me realize how few problems I had actually run into~~.  
  Later I gradually found more advanced videos and once again realized how little I knew.

- Now - 2024.07.31  
  I do not know whether the game has changed after all these years, or whether these tutorials still apply. But back then I was also reading articles from several years earlier, so perhaps experienced people can adapt what they need from my shallow notes.
